Daniel McKee

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign advised by Professor Svetlana Lazebnik. My research interests lie within computer vision, and I am especially interested in how we can learn models from video with limited annotations for recognition and tracking.

Before coming to UIUC I completed undergraduate degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics at Duke University.
